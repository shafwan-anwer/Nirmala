Welcome to Nirmala Hotel

The resturants and suites of Nirmala Hotel are diverse and unique. The Nirmala Hotel offers luxury accommodation among Sri Lanka resorts with breathtaking views of the Environment. Nestled amongst the wild coconut groves of Wadduwa, The Nirmala Hotel is truly a laid back paradise. The cool palms cast their shadow over the blue water, lit only by the last streaks of the setting sun, retreat from reality and surrender to the sublime serenity of the tropical island.
